Famy

Famy Bot.

Famy is a bot you can use daily to help you to find things you need or to do every day.

There are two ways to communicate with Famy. The first way, just say “HELLO FAMY” and then say what you need. The second way is to enter the app and write it. Famy is going to come with many features divided into multiple versions.

Features

On the first version Famy will come with 6 features:

Personalize Famy

You can personalize Famy in your own way & make it personal.

Chat Conversation

The application will contain a chat conversation that can help the bot to learn from you & know more things about you.

Playing Music

Famy can play music for you.

Searching For You

You can use Famy to search for anything for you just by Saying it.

Getting Location

Famy will be able to locate any services or places near to you using navigation & will send you the location with a description of that service or place.

Reminder

With Famy, you can create reminders only by selecting a date & time.

The Story Behind Famy

Our Story

Famy Bot is an AI project that simulates human conversations using NLP (natural language processing). the idea of Famy starts in 2020 from the founder Oussama Lakhdar who’s a young programmer and a student at 1337 school of 42 network, the project will be done with the help of other programmers. Famy will be done with AI & Deep Learning & python language. Python language makes use of a combination of Machine Learning algorithms in order to generate multiple types of responses, that’s will help Famy to communicate with humans & provide relevant & appropriate responses.
